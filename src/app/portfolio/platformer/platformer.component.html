In the procedural level, the game randomly generates platforms using predefined prefabs, in opposition to the traditional level, in which everything is laid out manually. Each platform has collectible entities, such as gems, lives and sometimes keys required to progress the level. Platforms may also have enemies patrolling them.

In the procedural level, the player is forced to always move to the right and can only jump or activate a special attack that launches a fireball to get rid of enemies that may be nearby.
